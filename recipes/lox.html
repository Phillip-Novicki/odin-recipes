Lox

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Fresh Wild Salmon
  • 1/2 cup Salt
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dill

Steps

  1. Pat down salmon with a paper towel and remove the moisture
  2. Add ingredients.
  3. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate.
  4. Rotate and drain every 12 hours (leave in the refrigerator for 3-5 days.
